CWA Email Template v1.0

A responsive email template with SalsaLabs merge tags in place. Based on Phil Wareham's Responsive Email template.

Images

Make sure you link all your images using full URL paths, and upload them to Salsa (ideal) or your webserver.

There are three image classes in this template (view the source to see how they are coded):

  1. banner - for the 600 px width banner image at the top of the email.
  2. half - for images that you want to display at half the width of a phone screen (good for floating text around the image). These images will be resized to a width of 150px.
  3. full - for images that you want to display at the full width of a phone screen. These images will be resized to a width of 280px.

Note: If you have an image that is wider than 280px and you do not give it one of these classes, it will probably not resize (and may break the email format) when viewed on a phone.
